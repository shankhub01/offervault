# OfferVault AI

AI-powered Affiliate Offer CRM & Intelligence Platform

Automatically extract affiliate offers from Gmail and Outlook, manage advertisers and publishers, track payouts, calculate margins, monitor expiry dates, and centralize campaign operations in one dashboard.

## Features

- AI Email Parsing
- Advertiser CRM
- Publisher Management
- Offer Tracking
- Margin Calculation
- Expiry Alerts
- Gmail Integration
- Outlook Integration
- Dashboard Analytics

## Target Users

- Affiliate Networks
- CPA Networks
- Media Buyers
- Affiliate Managers
- Business Development Teams
